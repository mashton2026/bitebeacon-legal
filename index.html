BiteBeacon Privacy Policy

Effective: March 2026

Who We Are

BiteBeacon is operated by BiteBeacon. For any questions, contact support@bitebeacon.uk.

Data We Collect

  • Account information (email, login details)
  • Vendor and listing data
  • Usage data (views, interactions)
  • Approximate location data (if enabled)

How We Use Data

  • To operate and improve the platform
  • To show nearby vendors
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse

Payments

Payments are processed by third-party providers such as Stripe.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting support@bitebeacon.uk.

Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly within the BiteBeacon app via the Security settings, or by contacting support@bitebeacon.uk.

Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and maintain platform security.

Contact
